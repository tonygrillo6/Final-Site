Pricing and Customizations

All our tee shirts come for the price range of $10.99 to $15.99 plus shipping and handling. Size will also play into cost. A large will be more expensive, while a small will be the cheapest. You can expect a your custom shirts in a resonable period of time and similar to sites like H & M and Hot Topic you can keep track of the delivery. We have a money back guarantee. If the ship order does not match the product, you will recieve a refund as well as a coupon for a free shirt with your next order. This is the vision our founder had back in the 80's, to make customizable tshirts for common people that were affordable and easy to get a hold of.

Additionally you will be able to place you size, address, and payment method below. This will reduce the amount of time needed on this site when buying your tee. You will also be able to include anything we should know about your customization for the shirt.
