About Us

We are a regional t shirt company that makes shirts and other articles of clothing for any season or event. We have shirts for every age group and we are sure you'll find something that screams you, not literally. Any size, any shape we've got you covered. Colors and designs may vary. Need a shirt with a princess or a race car for your little guy and gal, we've got you covered.We are a family friendly company, so keep that in mind when sending graphics for your custom tees.Founded in 1985, By Anthony Saperstien, better known as Tony, sought to find a better way to customize tshirts. This came to a head when he recieved an incorrect Star Wars tshirt with two pictures of Han Solo instead of Luke Skywalker. "This has to stop!!" He proclaimed and started his own business that made customizable tshirts for the common man at an affordable price.
